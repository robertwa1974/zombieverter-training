H01 — POWER

Power RequirementsPermanent 12V — not ignition switched

The Two Power Pins

PinFunctionNotes
Pin 5612V Permanent +Always on, even when vehicle off
Pin 5512V GroundChassis ground — clean connection required
SpecValue
Supply voltage12V DC
Idle current draw~150 mA
Bench supply setting13V, limit 1–2A
150mA idle

Comparable to a small relay module. Won't cause meaningful parasitic drain on a healthy 12V battery.

Why Permanent Power?

Charge timer management
VCU must stay on when parked to manage scheduled charging
ISA shunt continuity
Shunt must also have permanent power — same feed as VCU. Losing power loses UDC history.
Precharge reliability
Stale or zero UDC on startup causes precharge failures
🔴 Do Not

Do not use an ignition-switched supply for Pin 56. The VCU and ISA shunt must both have always-on power.

🔴 Critical wiring rule

Contactor outputs are low-side switching only. They pull the GROUND leg of the coil to ground. The positive leg of every coil must connect to 12V+. Never connect 12V+ to these pins.

Connector sourcing

56-pin Aptiv (formerly Delphi) connector. Available on AliExpress as full kit with housing, header, and pins.

🔴 Common mistake

The web UI lets you assign any function to any pin — even incompatible combinations. The hardware will silently ignore invalid assignments. Always verify pin type matches function type.
