🔴 Do NOT use dirmode for reverse

The GS450h has a physical reverse gear. Do not configure VCU forward/reverse pins for motor direction reversal — the transmission itself handles reverse via the neutral safety switch.

Connectivity check

With sync serial correctly connected, the temphs (inverter heat sink temperature) spot value will show a plausible room temperature (e.g. 25–35°C). This is your connectivity verification — no HV needed.

⚠ Gen3 vs Gen4 harness

Gen 3 (2007–2011) and Gen 4 (2013+) harnesses differ. Gen 3 is the primary supported conversion platform as of current firmware.

Fuse starting point

Start with 1A fuse and increase if it blows (2A → 3A → 5A). The GS450h inverter draws more than the VCU at startup.

⚠ Oil pump

The GS450h requires active coolant/oil pump when the inverter is operating. Assign the GS450Hpump function to the Pump PWM output pin. The pump must run during operation to prevent overheating.

I03 — HARNESS & DIAGNOSIS

Wire Harness & DiagnosisUse OEM harnesses where possible

What to Extract from OEM Harness

WhatUsed for
MG1 resolver cableMotor position sensing
MG2 resolver cableMotor position sensing
Shift solenoid harness (SL1, SL2)Transmission gear control
Neutral safety switch harnessP/R/N/D detection
You'll use ~12% of the harness

The rest is OEM engine/hybrid control wiring that can be discarded. Focus on resolver plugs and neutral safety switch branch.

⚠ Resolver cable handling

Use OEM shielded twisted pairs for sync serial signals — building from scratch is achievable but error-prone. Buy the OEM harness and extract what you need.
